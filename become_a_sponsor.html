Sponsoring the Titanium Titans

Sponsoring the Titanium Titans is a great way to support STEM education and technology workforce development in Western Pennsylvania. One hundred percent of all donations go towards registration fees, robot parts, materials, and student lodging during travel to competitions. All donors are recognized in a variety of ways.
